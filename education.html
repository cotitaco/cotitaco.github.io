Volunteering

Heart Of the City Piano Program Ottawa

This is a volunteering student association present in multiple cities around Canada. Our main mission is to provide elementary school kids with quality piano lessons for free. Our volunteers are in charge of coordinating with schools and also providing the piano lessons both online and in person. Our association also plans Coffee houses every term, where more volunteers do casual performances and we ask for sponsorships to raise founds and buy books and insentives for our students.

Executive Member

In this position my responsabilities are to help organize events such as the coffee houses, promote our values inside and outside the school and look for new volunteers.
