Who am I?

I recently concluded my Master's Degree at the University of Ottawa in Computer Science with a Concentration in Artificial Intelligence (GPA 3.91/4). My graduation project focuses on CyberSecurity, specifically how to integrate Large Language Models with traditional Machine Learning algorithms to analyze both system logs and raw IP packets to improve the prediction of malicious cyberattacks. I studied for a bachelor's Degree in Electronics and Telecommunications. A couple of years ago, I had the opportunity to work with a multinational Financial Institution in the technology sector for four years. interest 1

NLP/ML in CyberSecurity

As my Master's final project, I developed an ensemble model which consists of a DistilBERT / BERT pre-trained and fine-tuned model to extract relevant features from system logs. Together with a Random Forest Binary Classifier trained to analyze raw IP packets and detect DNS attacks. Resulting in a hard voting algorithm which improves the prediction of possible Denial of Service attacks.
